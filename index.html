About the game

how is the game?

The Legend of the greenhat man

A 2D puzzle adventure game in which the player controls the character to kill monsters, solve push-box puzzles, and kill the final boss. Players move horizontally and vertically in a locked 2D map to solve puzzles and/or kill monsters to unlock doors to other maps. After a series of adventure, the player will encounter the boss. The player have to ultimately defeat the final boss to win the game.
